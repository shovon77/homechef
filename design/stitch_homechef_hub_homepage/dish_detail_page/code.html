This isn't just any lasagna; it's a cherished family recipe passed down through three generations of Rossi women. My Nonna Elena brought this recipe with her from Bologna, Italy, and taught my mother, who then taught me. Each layer is crafted with love and the finest ingredients available.

We start with a slow-simmered ragù alla Bolognese, a rich and hearty meat sauce made with a blend of beef and pork, San Marzano tomatoes, and a secret blend of herbs. This is layered between delicate sheets of fresh, homemade egg pasta. Our béchamel sauce is silky smooth, made with whole milk, a hint of nutmeg, and generous amounts of Parmigiano-Reggiano. The entire dish is topped with more cheese and baked until it's bubbly and golden brown, with those irresistible crispy edges everyone fights over.

It's more than just a meal; it's a story on a plate, a comforting hug from our family to yours. Perfect for a family dinner, a special occasion, or simply when you need a little taste of home.
